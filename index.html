"Why fly when you can dine?"
Zambia’s First Smart, Upcycled Aviation-Themed Restaurant

A Flagship Project by UrbanShift Technologies Ltd.

The Concept

The Problem

Livingstone attracts global tourists, but dining experiences remain conventional. Modern travelers demand immersive, tech-enabled, premium concepts that go beyond the traditional safari lodge.

The Solution

A fully renovated 20-passenger aircraft transformed into a fine-dining destination — where luxury hospitality, tourism, and technology intersect.

Guest Experience

First-Class Atmosphere

  • Exclusivity: Only 20 seats per “flight” to maintain a private-jet feel.
  • Design: Modern retrofitting that preserves the aviation soul while delivering five-star comfort.
  • Ambiance: Starlight ceilings, velvet booths, and warm cabin lighting for an intimate evening setting.

Cabin Classes

Luxury First — Access by Design

The brand remains premium, while access is structured like aviation: tiered experiences with different reservation windows and service depth.

First Class

Private dining flights, curated tasting menus, premium pairings, and priority boarding (reservations).

Business Class

Premium à la carte dining with flexible weekday access — refined service, signature dishes, elegant atmosphere.

Sky Lounge

Accessible luxury: cocktails, small plates, and social lounge seating — designed for locals without diluting the brand.

Powered by UrbanShift

The Tech Edge

We don’t just serve food — we orchestrate a premium guest journey end-to-end, powered by a digital guest management layer.

  • Smart Check-in: Contactless arrivals via QR-code boarding passes.
  • In-Flight Service: Real-time kitchen coordination and digital ordering via in-seat tablets.
  • Integration: Seamless connection with ZamRide for VIP airport transfers.

The Airport Passport

More Than Dining

A premium membership layer for repeat guests — turning the restaurant into a lifestyle destination with priority boarding and exclusive flights.

  • Priority Boarding: early access to reservations and peak “flight” windows
  • Destination Stamps: themed menus (Tokyo, Paris, Lusaka) with seasonal rotations
  • Invite-Only Flights: private tastings, brand collaborations, and special events

The Boarding Pass

A Culinary Journey

A destination-based menu that makes every meal a global journey — from “Takeoff” to “Cruising Altitude.”

  • Global Cuisines: dishes inspired by Tokyo, Paris, and Lusaka.
  • Tactile Experience: a premium “boarding pass” menu design that feels collectible.
  • Signature Flights: chef-led tasting routes for special nights.

  • Aspirational locals who value quality and atmosphere
